Number of patients with DVT - 7.5% = [A] Total number of DVT patients eligible for dual LMWH/VKA or Xarelto

  • INR clinic visit avoided3,7,8

    [E]

    INR clinic visits avoided = number of DVT patients eligible for VKA treatment × number of INR monitoring visits per patient

  • Nurse visit avoided9

    [G]

    Nurse assistance visits avoided = number of non-cancer DVT patients eligible for initial LMWH treatment × proportion of these patients treated by nurses in their own homes × mean number of LMWH injections administrated

  • Hospital bed days saved10

    [H]

    Hospital bed days saved = number of eligible DVT patients × proportion of DVT patients hospitalised × number of hospital bed days saved per DVT patient treated with Xarelto
